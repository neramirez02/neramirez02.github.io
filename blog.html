1/22/16

We were building a balloon tower.
It was 86 inches tall.

Hello everybody, my purpose for this blog is to write out the challenges/problems I get from my Accelerated Computer Science elective (ACS) and to look back at the problems, which gives me the skill and experience for later on during college or job. I chose to be in ACS because I want to explore and learn more about Computer Science early than being taught later on high school but I want to have the first experience of ACS so I know the skill already and apply it later on college or a job. What I hope that I can get out of this blog and elective is have the skill or experience early in life so I put this in my resume for a college or job. I also want to look back at this blog when Iâ€™m a Senior or a Freshmen in college to see how my elective was back then and to see my work I made as a Freshmen in High School.

In the picture to the right, we were given these instructions to build the tallest balloon tower in 10 minutes. The only materials that we had was balloons and masking tape. We thought this was going to be a competition and there was two groups. My group had an idea of having the base 4 balloons and tape 2 balloons from their ends. We built off of the base and added 2 balloons each time. We kept repeating this process and our tower went to 57 inches in 10 minutes. When the time finished, the other group didn't made much progress at all and ended up falling off of the table. When we went back to the tables, we had a discussion about how things went well and what things went wrong. When we were about to start another round, our teacher told us that he never said anything about competition or being in groups. We were all suprised and shocked about that because our group was eager to create the tallest tower and never thought about working altogether. When the second round started, we all worked together to build off to the 57 inch tower but we only had 5 minutes to make it taller so we merged our two towers and added more balloons to the top. After 5 minutes, we made our tower to be 86 inches tall. We were all proud about our success and how we all worked together as a team. This was the first activity we had yet there are more other challenges that we need to do but working together we can finish any problem that we face each day of the elective. Well, that's all for today's blog post.
