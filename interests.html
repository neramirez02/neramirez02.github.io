Fallout 4


I also got this game when it first released. It's a role-playing game where the game takes place in Boston and I can roam to any part of the game, whether if I'm doing a quest or not. In the game, I can also control the story, whether if I would like to do a quest or do a different quest. It's not like Call Of Duty or Metro Last Night that already make the story and have to complete it in that order. I can simply decide if I would like to take the quest or not, since I can go back to it. However, the decisions I take on main quests can drive the storyline to different things and might cancel out a different quest, since in the game there are factions. In the game, there is an extraordinary plot that takes in the future, where atomic bombs fell into cities and been survivors from the bombs. And the the thing that I mostly enjoy about to game is that I can kill and blow up people, animals, and creatures in many different ways, especially in V.A.T.S. since I could kill them in slow motion with all the blood spreading.
